# PoseLab Ambient Curation v1.0
## Suno AI Prompt Library

This document contains a curated list of 12 musical prompts designed to complement the PoseLab studio experience. Each prompt is engineered for Suno AI using high-fidelity keywords consistent with the Project 89 aesthetic.

---

### 1. "Silicon Drift" (Vapor-Tech Chill)
- **Style Keywords:** Vaporwave, Cyber-chill, 80s Analog Synth, Liquid Bass, DX7 Keys, Nostalgic, Mid-tempo.
- **Description:** A smooth, neon-lit track perfect for long posing sessions. Imagine a high-tech studio floating in a purple haze.
- **Prompt:** `[Instrumental] Mid-tempo Vaporwave, heavy DX7 FM-synth pads, warm analog moog bassline, gated reverb snare, cyber-chill aesthetic, nostalgic dream-pop atmosphere, liquid electronics.`

### 2. "Neural Loop" (Deep Focus Minimal)
- **Style Keywords:** Minimal Techno, Glitch, Ambient, 120 BPM, Pulsating, Clinical, Neural-link, Deep Focus.
- **Description:** A repetitive, rhythmic pulse that aids concentration without being distracting. Matches the "Neural-link" technical jargon of the app.
- **Prompt:** `[Instrumental] Minimalist techno pulse, subtle glitch percussion, deep sub-bass hum, hypnotic 120 BPM, clinical precision, digital clockwork, neural-focus background ambiance.`

### 3. "Sakura Connectivity" (Lofi-Glitch)
- **Style Keywords:** Lofi Hip-hop, Bit-crushed, Glitch-hop, Emotional, Japanese Flute, Digital Sakura, Soft.
- **Description:** Blends organic Japanese instruments with digital artifacts. Soft and inviting for creative flow.
- **Prompt:** `[Instrumental] Lofi hip-hop beats, bit-crushed piano, ethereal shakuhachi flute accents, digital glitch textures, emotional and soft, Sakura bloom aesthetic, 85 BPM.`

### 4. "Azure Resonance" (Liquid DNB)
- **Style Keywords:** Liquid Drum and Bass, Atmospheric, Fast-paced, Smooth, Synthesizer, Deep Water, High Energy.
- **Description:** High-energy yet smooth. Best for rapid-fire "PoseLab Sprints" or testing high-speed animations.
- **Prompt:** `[Instrumental] Liquid Drum & Bass, high-speed breaks, lush atmospheric pads, rolling bassline, 174 BPM, azure depth, sophisticated synth melodies, airy and spacious.`

### 5. "Steel Integrity" (Industrial Ambient)
- **Style Keywords:** Industrial, Dark Ambient, Metallic, Reverb-heavy, Cinematic, Dystopian, Cyberpunk.
- **Description:** For the more serious, metallic, or mechanical characters. Harder edges but still ambient.
- **Prompt:** `[Instrumental] Dark industrial ambient, metallic percussion hits, deep drones, dystopian cinematic atmosphere, heavy reverb, steel factory echoes, futuristic brooding tone.`

### 6. "Magenta Frequency" (Synthwave Drive)
- **Style Keywords:** Retrowave, Outrun, Arpeggiated, 110 BPM, Driving, Neon, 1989, Energetic.
- **Description:** A driving beat that feels like traveling through the recursive loops of the 2089 network.
- **Prompt:** `[Instrumental] 80s Synthwave outrun, fast arpeggiated bass, bright polysynths, driving 4/4 beat, neon lights, retro-futuristic energy, 110 BPM, 1989 digital core.`

### 7. "Recursive Loop" (Math-Rock Lofi)
- **Style Keywords:** Math-rock, Clean Electric Guitar, Complex Rhythms, Lofi, Tapping, Intricate.
- **Description:** For the technical user. Intricate clean guitar lines that loop recursively.
- **Prompt:** `[Instrumental] Lofi math-rock, clean twinkly electric guitars, intricate finger-tapping patterns, complex odd-time signatures, chill jazz drums, recursive melodic loops.`

### 8. "The Green Loom" (Organic-Tech Folk)
- **Style Keywords:** Folktronica, Acoustic Guitar, Digital Synthesis, Nature Sounds, Hybrid, Peaceful.
